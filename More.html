EXO's Lightstick Info

    For their 2nd tour EXO'luXion, EXO revealed their very first official light stick, available for fans to purchase on October 2015. Less than a year later, a second version of the highly aesthetic light stick will be release to fans on July 2016. The first version was a very sleek black with EXO's logo at the top while the upcoming second version will be a white version of the original. While fans have left comments in awe and want for the new light stick, they have also left complaints over the price, asking SM Entertainment to lower the price. The new light stick is said to be priced between 25,000 won and 32,000 won, or $22 to $28 USD.
